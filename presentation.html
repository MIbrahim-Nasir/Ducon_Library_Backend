03 — The AI

The AI Does
Two Things,
Not One

Previously the AI worked from a single, fixed set of instructions for every image — like a camera with no settings. Now it thinks first, then creates. The result is a generation that's tailored to what's actually in the photos.

🧠
Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite
Analyses the images and writes the brief
🎨
Nano Banana Pro
Generates the photorealistic image
🔍
Gemini Embedding v2
Powers the intelligent catalogue search

How it works, step by step

1
Customer uploads their photo
The system automatically cleans it up — converts iPhone formats, reduces file size, normalises the image so the AI gets perfect input regardless of the device used.
Automatic · No customer action needed
2
AI studies both images and writes a creative brief
Flash-Lite looks at both the Ducon product/project and the customer's space, then writes a detailed, expert brief for the image generator — tailored specifically to what it sees.
New: dynamic per-generation, not a fixed template
3
Nano Banana Pro generates the image
Google's premium image generation model uses that brief and both reference images to produce a photorealistic visualisation — preserving the customer's space while applying the Ducon design.
Physics-aware · Photorealistic · 4K capable
4
Saved and served instantly
The generated image is stored in Cloudflare's global network and a secure link is sent back to the customer's app — their design history is always there when they return.
Cloudflare R2 · Presigned secure URL

07 — Technology

What Powers It All

The technologies we use — explained without jargon.

⚡
FastAPI
The backbone of the backend — handles every request the app sends and returns the right response, fast.
"Like a smart switchboard operator for the app"
🗄
PostgreSQL
Stores everything that matters — user accounts, bookmarks, generation history, catalogue records.
"The filing cabinet that never loses anything"
🔵
ChromaDB
A special database built for AI search — stores the "meaning fingerprints" of every catalogue image so they can be searched by similarity.
"A librarian who understands concepts, not just titles"
☁️
Cloudflare R2
Cloud storage for all generated images and the catalogue. Images load fast globally — and there's no data transfer cost.
"A photo album stored in the cloud, delivered anywhere"
🎨
Nano Banana Pro
Google's premium AI image generation model. Creates photorealistic visualisations by understanding both reference images and the written brief.
"A professional 3D artist who works in seconds"
🧠
Gemini Flash-Lite
A fast, cost-efficient Google AI model that reads both images and writes the expert creative brief before the image is generated.
"A creative director who briefs the artist"
🔬
Gemini Embeddings
Google's model that converts images and text into numerical "fingerprints" that capture their meaning — powering the smart catalogue search.
"DNA testing, but for images and descriptions"
🔐
Google OAuth + JWT
Secure, token-based login. "Sign in with Google" works instantly. Returning customers stay logged in safely without re-entering passwords.
"A digital keycard that expires and auto-renews"
