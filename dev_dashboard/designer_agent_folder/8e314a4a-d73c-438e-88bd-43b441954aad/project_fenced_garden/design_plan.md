# Fenced Garden Design Plan
## Space Analysis
- **Camera**: Elevated 45-60° angle from upper-left corner, looking toward back-right
- **Space**: Rectangular fenced area ~6×8m approx, with MATURE TREE in center-back zone
- **Fixed**: Large tree trunk (NEVER move/remove), beige compound wall background, dark green metal gate (right), house facade (left), existing brick paver driveway surrounding area
- **White picket fence**: Can be replaced by Ducon planter walls/blocks as boundary
- **Mesh/shed**: Small structure back-left corner (can redesign or hide with planting)
- **Constraints**: Design ONLY inside fence. Tree is anchor element.

## 3 Design Directions × 3 Variations Each = 9 Generations

---

### DIRECTION A: "THE SANCTUARY OF THE TREE" — Raised Planter Ring + Artisan Floor + Firepit Social
Philosophy: Frederick Law Olmsted (harmony with nature) + Roberto Burle Marx (organic bold forms)
- Central tree becomes a HERO — raised stone planter ring (Ducon blocks) around tree base
- Artisan geometric floor pattern radiating outward from tree (id 174, 300)
- Corner raised planter beds with flowering shrubs (id 238)
- Front social zone: firepit with built-in Ducon seating (id 110, 289)

Variation A1: Centered geometric artisan floor + square raised tree planter + circular firepit corner
Variation A2: Diagonal artisan floor lines + hexagonal tree planter + L-shaped seating with firepit
Variation A3: Organic curved paths + rounded raised planters + scattered seating vignettes

---

### DIRECTION B: "THE PERGOLA GROVE" — Wooden Pergola + Lounge Furniture + Lush Planters
Philosophy: Thomas Church (outdoor rooms) + Piet Oudolf (naturalistic planting)
- Ducon wooden pergola positioned to left/front of tree (not blocking canopy)
- Premium outdoor sofa lounge underneath (id 122, 123)
- Raised Ducon planter walls along fence perimeter with lush plantings (id 172)
- Tree gets a beautiful stone planter ring (id 26)
- Textured stone tile floor (id 106)

Variation B1: Full pergola front-half, tree featured in back with raised ring, sofa lounge
Variation B2: L-shaped pergola hugging left fence + planter walls + wicker chairs
Variation B3: Corner pergola with hanging seat + central paved floor + wrap planters

---

### DIRECTION C: "THE ZEN WATER GARDEN" — Water Feature + Minimal Paving + Lush Green
Philosophy: Peter Walker (minimalism) + James Corner (ecology + poetry)
- Ducon water feature/fountain near tree (id 85, 205)
- Clean large-format stone floor tiles with grass strips (id 58)
- Low Ducon planter blocks as fence replacement with dense flowering shrubs
- White/cream seating bench facing water feature under tree canopy
- Tree base surrounded by water channel or low planter (id 85)

Variation C1: Rectangular still water basin at tree base + large tile floor + bench seating
Variation C2: Cascading water wall on back fence + artisan floor centre + planter border ring
Variation C3: Stepped tiered planter fountain structure + mixed paving + curved seating wall
