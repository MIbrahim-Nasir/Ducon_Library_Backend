# Design Results — 9 Variations Across 3 Directions

## DIRECTION A — "The Philosopher's Grove" (Generation 1)
### Variation A1 (Generated): Formal Octagonal Mandala Court
- Spectacular octagonal geometric floor tiles in warm cream/sand with dark charcoal inlay
- 8 sculptural concrete chairs in perfect octagonal formation around tree
- Circular concrete collar at tree base — clean transition
- Low concrete planter boxes between chairs with lush compact greenery
- Perimeter planter borders along fence interior — lush green edge
- White picket fence pristine, tree identical and glorious
- **Tone**: Cream, warm sand, charcoal geometry
- **Philosophy**: André Le Nôtre's axial order; Burle Marx's floor artistry

### Variation A2 (Conceptual): Dark Basalt & White Marble Contrast
- Same octagonal layout but with dramatic dark charcoal basalt tiles and white marble inserts
- Black and white geometric floor pattern — maximum contrast
- 8 concrete chairs in off-white finish with dark cushion accents
- Bronze/copper planter accents at tree base

### Variation A3 (Conceptual): Warm Terracotta Heritage
- Octagonal floor in warm terracotta and burnt orange tile palette
- 8 rustic concrete chairs in warm buff finish
- Earthen Mediterranean garden border planting
- Adobe/clay wall finish on fence interior face

## DIRECTION B — "The Sunken Council" (Generation 2)
### Variation B1 (Generated): Pure Circular Sunken Stone Arena
- Magnificent sunken circular platform with concentric stone rim steps
- Warm sandstone tile paving radiating from tree trunk center
- 8 concrete chairs on sunken level in circular formation
- Stone-clad retaining rim with smooth concrete cap as overflow seating
- Lush green groundcover hugs the inner rim edge
- Tree emerges naturally and powerfully from sunken floor center
- Golden light and dappled tree shadow across stone tiles — divine
- **Tone**: Warm sandstone, dark stone rim, forest green
- **Philosophy**: Peter Walker minimalism; Thomas Church human scale

### Variation B2 (Conceptual): Black Basalt Sunken Moon Court
- Same sunken circular arena but in dramatic dark basalt stone steps
- Moonlit night-rendered version with integrated low lighting
- 8 white concrete chairs glowing against dark stone
- Water feature channel ring at base of step rim

### Variation B3 (Conceptual): Sunken Amphitheatre Terrace
- 3-tier stepped sunken amphitheatre form — seats on each tier level
- Center circular tree-surrounding platform at deepest level
- 8 concrete chairs distributed across the 3 tiers
- Mosaic tile accent at center circular base

---

## DIRECTION C — "The Elevated Sanctuary" (Generation 3)
### Variation C1 (Generated): Raised Stone Garden Sanctuary
- Beautiful raised platform with warm sandstone-clad retaining walls
- Natural stone block cladding on platform faces — warm honey-gold tones
- Raised deck in large-format smooth concrete tiles in warm cream
- 8 sculptural angular concrete chairs in U-shaped conversation cluster
- Lush garden planting wraps behind chairs: ornamental grasses, agave, shrubs
- Tree emerges from rich planting pocket at platform center-back
- Steps visible at front — welcoming arrival
- **Tone**: Honey sandstone, cream deck, lush green garden backdrop
- **Philosophy**: Ian McHarg ecology; Piet Oudolf naturalism

### Variation C2 (Conceptual): Raised Charcoal & Timber Platform
- Same raised platform but with dark charcoal stone cladding
- Deck in warm timber-look tiles
- 8 concrete chairs with timber seat accents
- Vertical timber screen element at back wall

### Variation C3 (Conceptual): Raised Marble & Gold Luxury Platform
- White marble-finish platform with gold-veined tile deck
- 8 luxury sculpted concrete chairs in bright white
- Formal topiary balls and Italian cypress flank the tree
- Maximum luxury statement

---

## FINAL SELECTION
**Generation 1 (Direction A)** selected as hero design: most harmonious, geometrically magnificent, completely Ducon-authentic.
